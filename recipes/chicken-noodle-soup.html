the ingredients are as follows

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 8 large carrrots, peeled
  • one large head of celery
  • chicken broth
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • seasoning salt
  • bay leaves
  • parsley
  • salt and pepper
  • wide egg noodles
  • Better than Bouillon brand chicken base
  • love muthafucka

Follow these steps to make this amazing ass soup

  1. slice chicken breasts into strips
  2. season your chicken generously, if you think you've added enough, add more
  3. brown your chicken in a frying pan until cooked through
  4. While the chicken is cooking, cut your carrots and celery into 1 inch pieces
  5. In a large stock pot, put yout chicken, vegetables, seasoning and chicken broth on a low simmer
  6. Leave this all to simmer for about an hour and a half, or until the vegetables are fork-tender
  7. At this point I know you are hungry as hell, so throw them noodles in there and wait until they are soft, then eat that thang
