The best mashed potatoes you will ever have!

These mashed potatoes are a huge hit! They are made with simple ingredients you probably have in your pantry already

Farm fresh milk and cheese, salty butter, delicious bacon and chives, Oh my!

This meal makes a perfect side to our crazy chicken tenders

These are the ingredients you need

  • 8 large russet potatoes
  • Whole milk, 1 cup
  • Heavy Cream, 1 cup
  • Salted butter, 2 sticks
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 1lb of thick cut bacon
  • As much green onion as your heart desires, really, go crazy if you want!
  • 2 cups of cheddar blend cheese

Follow these steps to cheesy,cheesy greatness

  1. Wash and peel your potatoes, or don't peel them, see if I care
  2. Now get some water(or chicken broth) boiling on the stove, make sure it is salty like the ocean
  3. Boil the potatoes until they are fork tender
  4. While the potatoes are boiling, cut your bacon into one inch slices and cook it to your desired doneness, we're a crispy bacon household here but really any kind will do
  5. Once those potatoes are done, mash them carefully, turning in your milk, cream, and butter until it is as creamy as you prefer. Season them to your taste
  6. Once your potatoes are mashed, put all the potatoes into a casserole dish
  7. Coat the potatoes with cheese, the crumbled bacon and green onions, as much as you like!
  8. Cook at 325 degrees until the cheese is melted and you're done!
